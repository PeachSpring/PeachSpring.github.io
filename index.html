Looking_Back

Posted on 2022-10-14

Looking Back is an caricature painted by 藤本樹. It’s about two girls’ story about their love for painting caricature. I’m an avid fan of 藤本樹 for his caricature and this is the first entity book I bought though I have liked him for many years.

This picture shows the heroine’s normal life. She always draws from spring to the next spring. In this picture, the heroine’s position is fixed the room settings can tell the time is flowing. Actually it’s so normal in 藤本樹’s work, he always express his idea by details which can convey the idea “silently”.

藤本樹 is a weird person, he is always called neuropathy by his fans. But “neuropathy” contains no hate. His anime is rambled but interesting and beautiful all the time. This can’t be showed by words, I sincerely commend you to read his caricature.
